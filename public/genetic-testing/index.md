# Genetic Testing

## Clinical Focus & Department Overview
Integrating molecular genetics into orthopedic diagnosis to identify hereditary risks for connective tissue disorders, muscular dystrophies, and baseline tendinopathy risks.

## Pathophysiology, Diagnostics & Procedures
Saliva and blood DNA sequencing, collagen gene mutation mapping, metabolic bone disorder diagnostics, and family risk counseling.
