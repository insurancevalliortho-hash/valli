# Sports Training

## Clinical Focus & Department Overview
Focused on optimizing athletic biomechanics, posture, and strength to build baseline physical resilience and minimize the incidence of non-contact structural injuries.

## Pathophysiology, Diagnostics & Procedures
Functional movement screening (FMS), gait analysis, plyometric and agility training, muscular balance testing, and personalized athletic development programs.
