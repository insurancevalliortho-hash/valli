# Fracture Clinic

## Clinical Focus & Department Overview
Operating under the philosophy that "Movement is Life," our team provides emergency and planned stabilization for simple, compound, and complex multi-fragment fractures.

## Pathophysiology, Diagnostics & Procedures
Open Reduction Internal Fixation (ORIF) with advanced locking plates, closed reductions and plaster casting, external fixation for open trauma, and limb-spanning pelvic stabilization.
