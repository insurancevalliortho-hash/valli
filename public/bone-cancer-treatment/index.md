# Bone Cancer Treatment

## Clinical Focus & Department Overview
Providing highly specialized Orthopedic Oncology care, focusing on the diagnosis, surgical resection, and reconstruction of primary and metastatic bone and soft tissue tumors.

## Pathophysiology, Diagnostics & Procedures
Tumor resections, limb-salvage surgeries using custom mega-prostheses, biological reconstructions with bone grafts, and multidisciplinary coordination for chemotherapy/radiotherapy.
