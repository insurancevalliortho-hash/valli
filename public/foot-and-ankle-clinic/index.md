# Foot and Ankle Clinic

## Clinical Focus & Department Overview
Dedicated to restoring full ambulation, managing complex congenital or acquired deformities, and salvaging compromised limbs to facilitate a rapid return to daily, pain-free activities.

## Pathophysiology, Diagnostics & Procedures
Surgical reconstructions, flatfoot and bunion corrections, tendon transfers, diabetic foot care, and complex ankle arthrodesis or ligament reconstructions.
